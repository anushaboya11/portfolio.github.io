Cybersecurity

Description:

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access, theft, damage,or any form of cyber-attacks. As our world becomes increasingly digitized and interconnected, the importance of cybersecurity has grown exponentially to safeguard sensitive information, personal data, financial transactions, and critical infrastructure from potential threats.
